Our Services

Comprehensive Healthcare Solutions

From nursing care to specialized treatments, we offer a full range of home healthcare services tailored to your needs.

General Nursing Care

Professional nursing services including wound care, injections, IV therapy, and vital monitoring.

  • Wound dressing
  • Medication management
  • Health monitoring

Additional Services:

  • IV therapy & injections
  • Vital signs monitoring
  • Blood sugar testing
  • Catheter care & management
  • Oxygen therapy support
  • Post-hospitalization care
Availability: Available 24/7 for emergency and scheduled visits

Home Health Aide

Personal care assistance with daily activities to maintain comfort and dignity at home.

  • Bathing & hygiene
  • Mobility assistance
  • Meal preparation

Additional Services:

  • Light housekeeping
  • Companionship & emotional support
  • Errand assistance
  • Grocery shopping
  • Laundry & clothing care
  • Transportation to appointments
Availability: Flexible scheduling - hourly, daily, or live-in options

Mother & Baby Care

Specialized post-natal care for new mothers and newborn babies in the comfort of your home.

  • Newborn care
  • Breastfeeding support
  • Mother's recovery

Additional Services:

  • Umbilical cord care & bathing
  • Sleep training guidance
  • Postpartum wellness monitoring
  • Baby massage techniques
  • Feeding schedule management
  • Mother's nutrition counseling
Availability: Day and night shifts available for first 40 days

Dementia & Elderly Care

Compassionate care for seniors with dementia, Alzheimer's, or age-related conditions.

  • Memory care
  • Safety supervision
  • Companionship

Additional Services:

  • Daily routine assistance
  • Fall prevention strategies
  • Medication reminders
  • Cognitive stimulation activities
  • Wandering prevention
  • Family respite care
Availability: 24-hour care options with trained dementia specialists

Post-Surgery Care

Recovery support after hospital discharge to ensure smooth healing and rehabilitation.

  • Wound management
  • Physical therapy
  • Recovery monitoring

Additional Services:

  • Pain management support
  • Mobility exercises
  • Nutrition guidance
  • Surgical site monitoring
  • Medication administration
  • Follow-up appointment coordination
Availability: Short-term and extended recovery programs available

Chronic Disease Management

Ongoing care for diabetes, hypertension, heart disease, and other chronic conditions.

  • Regular check-ups
  • Medication reminders
  • Diet guidance

Additional Services:

  • Blood pressure monitoring
  • Diabetes care & insulin management
  • Lifestyle counseling
  • Exercise programs
  • Health education
  • Emergency response planning
Availability: Weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly care plans
